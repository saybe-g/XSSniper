XSSNIPER — fast reflected XSS tester

options:
  -h, --help            show this help message and exit
  -u, --url URL         URL with injection point. Use FUZZ or just append at the end
  -f, --payloads-file PAYLOADS_FILE

  --color               Enable colored output (ANSI colors)
  -d, --delay DELAY     Delay between requests in seconds (default: 0.6)

  Don't use this flag if you're outputting the contents to a file. The output may look bad.
